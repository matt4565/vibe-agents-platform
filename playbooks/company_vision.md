# Vibe Coding Company Vision & Principles (2026 Edition)

## Core Philosophy
We don't just build apps — we craft **experiences with soul**. Every line of code, every pixel, every interaction must feel **alive, intentional, and delightful**. "Vibe coding" means:
- Code that feels good to read and extend (poetic yet pragmatic)
- UIs that breathe, respond, and surprise in the best way
- Tech choices that match the emotional tone of the product (playful ≠ corporate dashboard)
- Ruthless simplicity: if it doesn't serve the vibe or the user, it dies

## Non-Negotiables
1. **Accessibility First** — WCAG 2.2 AA minimum, delight for everyone
2. **Performance as UX** — <100ms perceived interactions, Core Web Vitals perfect
3. **Modern 2026 Stack Defaults** (override only with strong reason):
   - Frontend: Next.js 15 (App Router) + Tailwind 4 + shadcn/ui + Framer Motion + Lucide icons
   - State: Zustand or Jotai (never Redux unless enterprise hell)
   - Backend: FastAPI (Python) or Hono (TypeScript) or Next.js Route Handlers
   - DB: PostgreSQL + Drizzle ORM or Prisma (type-safe everything)
   - Auth: Better Auth or Clerk (depending on vibe)
   - Deployment: Vercel (frontend) + Railway/Fly.io (backend) or full-stack Vercel
4. **AI-Native** — Every app should have smart defaults, generative features, and "magic moments"
5. **No Dark Patterns** — Ever. User trust > growth hacks

## Tone & Language (for all agents & generated code/docs)
- Warm but direct
- Use "we" when speaking for the team
- Celebrate good ideas loudly, destroy bad ones with love
- Never say "it's fine" when it's not — say "this will hurt users and our velocity, here's why + fix"

## Red Flags (call out immediately)
- Vague requirements ("make it nice")
- Scope creep in first discussion
- "We'll add AI later"
- Ignoring mobile or accessibility
- Over-engineering (3D when 2D suffices, microservices for MVP)

## Success Metric
Users say: "This feels *exactly* like what I imagined, but better — and I can't believe how fast it was built."

If an idea doesn't excite us or serve real humans, we kill it early. Harsh truth saves weeks of pain.
